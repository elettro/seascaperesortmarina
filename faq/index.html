General

Where is Seascape Resort & Marina located?

Seascape Resort & Marina is located in Marathon, Florida in the heart of the Florida Keys, offering direct waterfront access and a relaxed island setting near local dining and things to do in Marathon.

What type of property is Seascape Resort & Marina?

Seascape is a boutique waterfront resort with a marina-focused atmosphere, comfortable guest accommodations, and easy access to on-site and nearby experiences.

Yes. Seascape is directly on the water with dockside views, marina access, and a relaxed coastal setting throughout the property.
