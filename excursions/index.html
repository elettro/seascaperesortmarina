Excursion Conditions & Visibility

Snorkeling Requirements

Snorkeling excursions are surface snorkeling experiences only and are not scuba diving tours. Guests should be able to swim comfortably in the ocean without assistance. Masks, snorkels, and fins are used during the excursion. Flotation aids are available upon request.

Water Visibility

Ocean conditions in the Florida Keys change frequently due to wind, tides, and seasonal weather patterns. Water clarity and visibility vary throughout the year and cannot be guaranteed.

Trip Adjustments

If conditions are not suitable for the planned activity, the trip may be adjusted to a different location or substituted with an alternative activity based on available conditions. Guests will be informed of conditions and available options before departure during the required captain check-in call.

Captain Decision & Refunds

Capt. Kevin always works to choose the best available location and safest conditions for each day’s trip. If the captain cancels a trip due to weather or poor operating conditions, your deposit or trip cost will be refunded in full. The captain makes the final decision to operate or cancel.

Booking & Availability

Excursions are recommended to be reserved at least 48 hours in advance.
