Seascape Resort & Marina

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Privacy Policy

Seascape Resort & Marina respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information when you browse our website, contact our team, or book your stay.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you contact us.
  • Reservation details and guest preferences when booking accommodations.
  • Billing and payment information processed through secure booking partners.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Confirm and manage reservations.
  • Respond to questions, requests, and support needs.
  • Provide property updates, policy notices, and important service communications.
  • Improve website functionality and guest experience.

Cookies and Analytics

Our site may use cookies and similar technologies to understand website performance, measure traffic, and enhance usability. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted providers (such as booking engines, payment processors, or analytics services) only as needed to operate our services and website.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No online transmission is completely secure, but we continuously review safeguards to reduce risk.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request updates or corrections to your personal information, or to opt out of non-essential communications.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
