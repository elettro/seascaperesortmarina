Main Strategic Goal

The objective after launch is not simply “having a website online.” The goal is transforming Seascape Resort & Marina into a stronger direct-booking destination with emotional differentiation from generic Florida Keys motel competitors. The marina lifestyle, relaxed social atmosphere, giant chair, cornhole area, pool adjacency, and visual personality are all valuable conversion assets that help guests emotionally remember the property before they ever arrive.
