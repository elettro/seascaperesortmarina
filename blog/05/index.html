TripAdvisor Review Breakdown: Why Seascape Resort & Marina Scores So Strong With Guests

Seascape Resort & Marina has earned a 4.6 "Very Good" rating on TripAdvisor, based on 836 reviews. Even stronger, it ranks #1 of 13 small hotels in Marathon.

That combination says a lot. Guests are not only leaving positive reviews. They are ranking Seascape above every comparable small hotel in the area.

For travelers choosing where to stay in the Florida Keys, review scores matter. They show what past guests experienced across the areas that shape a trip.

Location: 4.8

The 4.8 location score is the standout. This is the highest category rating shown, and it reflects one of Seascape's biggest advantages.

Guests value the setting. Seascape offers the classic Marathon experience: waterfront access, marina atmosphere, easy access to Keys attractions, and a relaxing resort environment without the feel of a massive hotel.

A high location score tells future guests they are booking into the right area, not settling for a disconnected hotel far from the water, boating, dining, and island activities.

Cleanliness: 4.7

Cleanliness is one of the most important hotel review categories. A 4.7 score shows guests consistently feel the property is well cared for.

For a small resort, this matters even more. Guests expect charm, comfort, and personality, but they also expect clean rooms, clean shared areas, and a property that feels maintained.

Service: 4.7

The 4.7 service rating is a major trust signal. Small hotels compete on personal attention. Guests often choose a smaller resort because they want a more relaxed, human experience than a large chain hotel provides.

This score suggests Seascape delivers that experience. Guests feel supported, welcomed, and taken care of.

Value: 4.5

A 4.5 value score means guests feel the stay is worth the price. That matters in the Florida Keys, where lodging costs rise quickly.

Guests want more than a room. They want location, atmosphere, amenities, convenience, and comfort. Seascape's value score shows guests see the full package.

Sleep Quality: 4.5

Sleep quality is often overlooked until it becomes a problem. A 4.5 score shows guests are generally resting well during their stay.

After a day in the sun, on the water, or exploring Marathon, comfort matters. Strong sleep quality means guests leave refreshed, not frustrated.

Rooms: 4.4

The rooms score is still strong at 4.4. This tells a clear story. Guests are pleased with the accommodations, while the resort's biggest strengths appear to be the total experience.

For a Florida Keys resort, that is important. Guests often choose properties like Seascape because they want more than a standard hotel room. They want outdoor space, water access, convenience, and a relaxed Keys atmosphere.

#1 of 13 Small Hotels in Marathon

This ranking is one of the strongest points in the review profile. Being rated #1 of 13 small hotels in Marathon means Seascape is not only performing well on its own. It is outperforming its direct category competitors.

For travelers comparing boutique hotels, waterfront stays, and smaller resort-style properties, this ranking gives Seascape a powerful advantage.
