Lead Capture

1. Phone System + Call Tracking

Turn every call into a measurable lead. Route inquiries fast. Build a cleaner guest follow-up system.

  • Separate call flows for rooms, marina, fishing, and snorkeling excursions.
  • Track call source so you know what content and campaigns generate bookings.
  • Capture caller data for return-stay offers and local staycation promotions.
  • Use voicemail-to-text or SMS follow-up for missed calls.
Occupancy Driver

2. Email Marketing for Last-Minute Openings

Use the email list as a rapid room-fill tool. This is strongest when aimed at people within driving distance of Marathon.

  • Lean into South Florida staycations from Jupiter, West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, Fort Lauderdale, Miami, and Homestead.
  • Position Seascape as a quick-drive tropical escape with dock sunsets, fishing, snorkeling, and relaxed marina energy.
  • Create a dedicated “48 Hour Keys Escape List” for guests willing to travel on short notice.
  • Trigger campaigns on cancellations, soft midweek occupancy, and unsold weekend inventory.
Pricing Strategy

3. Resort Fee as a Promotional Lever

Protect the room rate. Promote discounts or waivers on the resort fee to create visible savings without cutting core pricing.

  • Set a standard resort fee that supports the property’s amenities and guest experience.
  • Offer “Resort Fee Waived” for return guests, staycation specials, and last-minute bookings.
  • Use “50% Off Resort Fee” for midweek demand generation and direct-booking incentives.
  • Build perceived value while keeping the room positioned as premium inventory.
Reputation

4. Review Response Engine

Every review response should reinforce trust, direct booking, and repeat-stay value.

  • Respond to every Google and TripAdvisor review.
  • Thank guests and mention direct-booking advantages for future stays.
  • Use return-guest offers in selective responses when appropriate.
  • Turn public responses into micro-marketing moments for future guests.
Discovery Traffic

5. YouTube + Video Content System

Use video to attract travelers searching for the exact experiences Seascape sells.

  • Feature snorkeling outings, fishing trips, dock sunsets, marina mornings, and room walk-throughs.
  • Create short clips for Instagram Reels, Facebook, and YouTube Shorts.
  • Show the resort as a basecamp for adventure plus relaxation.
  • Use content both for ads and for organic search discovery.
Social Media

6. Social Content Pillars

Build a repeatable content framework so the resort always has something worth posting.

  • Resort life. Rooms, pool, docks, sunrises, sunsets, and marina views.
  • Ocean adventure. Snorkeling trips, fishing charters, boats leaving the dock, guest catches.
  • Local lifestyle. Restaurants, cocktails, cook-your-catch spots, and easy Keys evenings.
  • Promotional moments. Last-minute openings, fee-waived specials, and event nights.
In-Room Monetization

7. QR Code Guest Revenue System

Capture guest attention while they are already on property and in buying mode.

  • Place QR codes in rooms for merch, charter bookings, snorkeling reservations, and return-stay discounts.
  • Create a small mobile-friendly digital store tied to the guest stay.
  • Offer a future-booking incentive before guests check out.
  • Track scans and conversions to see which offers perform best.
Merchandising

8. Print-on-Demand Seascape Merch

Give guests something memorable to buy without taking on inventory headaches.

  • Create branded hats, fishing shirts, beach towels, stickers, tumblers, and marina lifestyle apparel.
  • Use room QR codes, social posts, and front desk prompts to drive purchases.
  • Pair product drops with seasonality, events, and excursion promotions.
  • Extend the Seascape brand beyond the stay itself.
New Revenue Stream

9. Private Label Coffee Program

Add a branded Seascape coffee line powered by Don Pablo Coffee, a highly regarded U.S. roaster with a reputation built around top-tier Arabica quality.

  • Offer Seascape private label coffee sourced through Don Pablo Coffee.
  • Position it as locally connected, premium, giftable, and tied to the Florida Keys stay experience.
  • Sell bags in-room through QR codes, at the front desk, online, and inside staycation promo bundles.
  • Create themed blends such as Dockside Sunrise Roast, Captain’s Morning Blend, or Sunset Marina Roast.
Partnerships

10. Influencer + Excursion Partnerships

Partner with people and operators who already fit the visual and lifestyle tone of the resort.

  • Collaborate with Captain Kevin for fishing and snorkeling packages plus real on-the-water content.
  • Host selected lifestyle influencers who naturally fit Keys travel and coastal content.
  • Create stay + charter and stay + snorkeling combinations for direct promotion.
  • Use influencer content as social proof and future ad creative.
Events

11. Weekend Entertainment + Community Energy

Give guests a reason to stay on property longer and give locals a reason to stop by.

  • Build sunset music nights, reggae nights, classic rock sets, and dockside DJ evenings.
  • Let excursion operators underwrite selected event nights.
  • Promote event weekends to the same South Florida staycation list.
  • Turn entertainment into content, not only into a one-night happening.
Packages

12. Stay + Experience Packages

Package the Seascape stay around what people want to do in Marathon, not only where they sleep.

  • Fishing weekends with room, charter, fish cleaning, and cook-your-catch guidance.
  • Snorkeling adventure getaways with room, guided outing, and sunset dock time.
  • Church retreat and group weekends with simple bundled pricing.
  • South Florida drive-down bundles with waived or discounted resort fees.

Rollout

Suggested implementation timeline.

Simple phased execution keeps the plan realistic while still moving quickly.

Phase 01

Capture and organize

Set up call tracking, guest list segmentation, review response workflow, and QR concept structure.

Phase 02

Launch demand drivers

Deploy last-minute staycation emails, Stories, direct-booking promos, and resort fee-waiver offers.

Phase 03

Monetize the stay

Push merch, private label coffee, package sales, and in-room future-booking incentives.

Phase 04

Scale the brand

Expand content production, partnerships, events, YouTube discovery, and recurring local audience campaigns.

Messaging angle

Position Seascape as the quick-drive Keys reset.

Do not market the property like a generic hotel. Market it as a calm marina basecamp with sunset atmosphere, easy access to fishing and snorkeling, and a much faster path to the Keys experience.

  • A quick drive to paradise.
  • Trade traffic for dock sunsets.
  • Stay, fish, snorkel, unwind.

Positioning summary

Seascape should sell more than rooms. It should sell an easy Keys experience.

The strongest version of this resort brand is not only lodging. It is direct-booked weekend escapes, marina lifestyle energy, fishing and snorkeling access, in-room upsells, private label coffee, event moments, and repeat visits driven by smart last-minute email marketing.

  • Last-minute email fills
  • Excursion package revenue
  • Coffee and merch sales
  • Repeat guest retention
